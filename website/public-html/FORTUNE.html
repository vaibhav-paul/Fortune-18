ABOUT FORTUNE'18

The Accounting Skills and Stock Exchange Training Society (ASSETS) of DTU introduces Fortune'18 as its annual financial and economic fiesta, slated to be organized on 20th-21st March this year. With an aim to attract the best brains and give them a common platform to compete, it will allow the students to test various abilities such as leadership qualities, synergy, team work skills, initiative, interpersonal skills and many more. Fully aware of the engineering background that the university proudly boasts of, ASSETS believes that interest has no boundaries and the determination to pursue counts the most. Our goal is to widen our reach so as to be able to provide a platform for the young, talented, budding economists to test, exhibit and hone their financial knowledge and skills. Fortune will provide you with opportunities to implement your ideas and vision relating to finance and economics with the help of some fun-filled and brain-storming events. This fiesta is delineated to emulate the corporate world, recreating real-time business scenarios and situations to analyze the participants and find the best among the competitors. It had its beginnings in a small room with an even smaller group of people, people who wanted to explore the economic and financial realm outside of textbooks and supply-demand graphs. And now, moving out of that small room, they invite students to join them on this exploration, to experience the exhilaration of having the world's welfare riding on your shoulders, the rush of being in the centre of the rise and fall of money.

AD MAD

Ad Mad is an ad making competition for students to showcase their creativity and innovativeness in marketing products. Its objective is to acquaint the students about promotional strategies in marketing through advertisements. Out of the box thinking is the key to earning brownie points!

Rules and Regulations

1. Participant should be single.
2. Time allotted will be a maximum of 3 minutes.
3. The decision of the judge will be final.

POLISELF

Ever thought about how you would do as captain of the ship? The wheel’s in your hands, the course is yours to chart. Economic crisis is looming large, and YOU have to be the one to solve it. Participants must come up with a policy to defuse (or solve, if you must) the issue at hand. The given problem may vary from a microeconomic issue like a staff strike at your college campus, to quintessential macroeconomic problems, like the solution to world hunger.

Rules and Regulations

1. Participant should be single.
2. Time allotted will be a maximum of 5 minutes to come up with a new policy/solution to the problem.
3. The designated economic problem will be allotted through chit-picking.
4. The decision of the judge will be final.

Venue – OAT/Amul Ground

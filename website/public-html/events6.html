STAKEHOLDER’S MEET

The Stakeholders meet is a meeting/conference where representatives or as the name suggests stakeholders from different organizations around the world meet and deliberate over a pressing issue that impacts their organizations. A complete analysis of the issue along with a solution- oriented approach is proposed to be carried out in this particular meeting for a fruitful outcome. A Stakeholders’ meet is a public meeting which implies all the discussions/solutions/documents that the delegates present are to be shared with the general public too which makes the representatives of these organisations accountable and responsible to the people.
